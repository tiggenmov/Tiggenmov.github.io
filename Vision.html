VISION & FUTURE

Building the Sovereign Tigray of Tomorrow

Our vision is not just survival—it's thriving. It's a Tigray that controls its destiny, secures its people, and builds prosperity for generations to come.

The Sovereign Framework

1. SECURITY AUTONOMY

A professional Tigray Territorial Defense Force under civilian oversight. Control of borders, airspace, and internal security. No foreign or federal troops without consent.

2. ECONOMIC SOVEREIGNTY

Control over natural resources, taxation, trade, and monetary policy. Reconstruction funded through Tigray bonds, diaspora investment, and transparent resource management.

3. POLITICAL SELF-RULE

A confederal relationship with Ethiopia where Tigray controls its constitution, judiciary, education, health, and foreign trade relations. Or full independence through referendum.

The Generational Covenant

"We pledge to build a Tigray where no child knows famine, where no mother fears soldiers, where no elder dies displaced, and where every generation adds a stone to the fortress of our sovereignty. This is our epoch. This is our covenant."

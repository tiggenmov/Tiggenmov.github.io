PREAMBLE (2025 Edition)

We, the surviving generation of Tigray, witnessing the fifth year since the genocide began in 2020,

Having documented over 500,000 deaths, systematic rape as weapon of war, and deliberate starvation of 6 million people,

Recognizing that impunity continues while justice delays, and that our political autonomy remains suppressed,

Do hereby reaffirm and strengthen the Tigray Generational Movement for the year 2025 and beyond.

ARTICLE I: THE 2025 TRUTH WE UPHOLD

1. We affirm that the Ethiopian and Eritrean states committed genocide (2020-2022) and continue crimes against humanity through siege and occupation.

2. We commit to 2025 as the year of maximal evidence collection, with 10,000 new testimonies targeted.

3. We reject any "reconciliation" without justice, any "dialogue" without sovereignty, any "peace" without freedom.

ARTICLE II: THE 2025 JUSTICE WE PURSUE

1. We demand ICC arrest warrants for 12 named perpetrators by December 2025.

2. We demand UN-supervised referendum on Tigray's political status by 2026.

3. We demand immediate lifting of all sieges, free movement, and return of 2.5 million displaced people.

ARTICLE III: THE 2025 SOVEREIGNTY WE BUILD

1. We assert that 2025 must see concrete steps toward Tigray's self-administration.

2. We commit to building parallel institutions: security, economy, judiciary, and diplomacy.

3. We envision a sovereign Tigray as a member of the United Nations by 2030.

ARTICLE IV: THE 2025 GENERATIONAL STRATEGY

1. We adopt "Three Fronts" for 2025: Legal (ICC), Political (international pressure), Ground (community resilience).

2. We prioritize youth leadership (under 35) in 60% of movement positions.

3. We establish the "2025 Tigray Solidarity Fund" to support survivors and resistance.

2025 DECLARATION OF URGENCY

Time is against us but truth is with us.

Five years of suffering must become zero years of impunity.

PREAMBLE: OUR INTERNAL REALITY

The Tigrayan people have endured a system that promised liberation but delivered betrayal.

A corrupt and self-serving political machinery — "The System" — prioritized its survival over the people's survival, its wealth over the people's poverty, and its power over the people's voice.

We, the Change Generation, are the children of this betrayal. This is not about external actors. This is about taking radical responsibility for our own house.

TIGRAY 2 IS OUR INTERNAL REVOLUTION. IT BEGINS WITH US.

THE SEVEN CORE PRINCIPLES

1

THE PEOPLE ARE THE SOVEREIGN

  • The absolute source of power and legitimacy is the Tigrayan people.
  • The System is a temporary servant, not a master.
  • Any power not freely granted by the people is theft.
2

UNCONDITIONAL BREAK WITH CORRUPTION

  • No secret deals, no elite cartels, no politics of lies.
  • No permanent excuse of "struggle" to justify corruption or authoritarianism.
  • The sacrifice of youth for elite gain ends permanently.
3

THE ONE-TIME DOOR – FORGIVENESS WITHOUT REVENGE

  • A historic, one-time offer to break the cycle of violence.
  • Full surrender of corrupt power earns forgiveness.
  • No revenge, no lynching, no collective punishment.
  • Betrayal repeated means the door closes forever.
4

JUSTICE AND ACCOUNTABILITY

  • Accountability for corruption and abuse of trust is non-negotiable.
  • Truth about internal crimes must be publicly established.
  • No future can be built on impunity.
5

POWER WITH LIMITS

  • Strict term limits for all leadership positions.
  • Independent judiciary and anti-corruption institutions.
  • Full transparency of public resources.
  • People's right to recall failed leaders.
6

THE CHANGE GENERATION LEADS

  • Youth, professionals, displaced people, and citizens are the architects.
  • New ideas, new faces, real competition.
  • Dissent is patriotic. Debate is strength.
7

LIFE AND DIGNITY ARE SACRED

  • No political goal justifies civilian suffering.
  • Protecting life, home, and future is the highest priority.

OUR INTERNAL COMMITMENT

NON-VIOLENCE

We will not become what we oppose.

COURAGE

We speak truth to internal power.

CLARITY

We clean our house first.

RESPONSIBILITY

Our future is in our hands.

STAND WITH TIGRAY 2

Support the internal renewal of Tigray. Add your voice to the Change Generation.

Tigray 2 begins now. It begins with us.

Critical Truths of the Youth of Tigray

For more than 50 years, generation after generation of Tigrayan youth have endured systematic suffering under the Ethiopian state. We were promised change, peace, and equality—but instead, we inherited cycles of repression, war, and targeted violence.

WE DEMAND TRUTH

We refuse silence. We seek full acknowledgment of the crimes committed against our people, without distortion, denial, or political manipulation.

WE DEMAND PEACE

A peace built on justice, accountability, and the freedom for Tigray to determine its own future without fear or coercion.

WE DEMAND REHABILITATION

Rebuilding our destroyed homeland—schools, hospitals, communities, identity, and future. Trauma healing, economic restoration, and support for survivors must be at the center.

A GENERATION THAT SURVIVED

We grew up through siege, famine, digital blackout, and genocide. We carry both the scars and the determination to never allow this to happen again.

OUR FUTURE IS SOVEREIGNTY

The youth of Tigray believe in a future where our people live free from imposed suffering, free from fear, and free to build a peaceful, democratic, sovereign nation.

We are the generation that refuses erasure. We are the generation that turns trauma into transformation.

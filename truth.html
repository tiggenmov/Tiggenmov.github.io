TRUTH ARCHIVE

Documenting evidence, preserving testimonies, and securing truth for justice and future generations.

Why We Document Truth

Truth is the foundation of justice. Without accurate documentation of what happened, justice cannot be served, and history becomes vulnerable to erasure. The Tigray 2 Truth Archive serves as a permanent digital repository for evidence, testimonies, and documentation—ensuring that future generations know what truly occurred and that accountability remains possible.

What We Document

Testimonies

First-hand accounts from survivors, witnesses, and affected communities. All testimonies are anonymized for safety.

Evidence

Photographs, videos, satellite imagery, and other visual evidence documenting events and violations.

Reports

Statistical data, human rights reports, and analysis from credible organizations and researchers.

Media Coverage

News articles, investigative journalism, and international media reports for historical context.

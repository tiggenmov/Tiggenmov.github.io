THE YOUTH OF TIGRAY

We are the generation that refuses silence. We build Tigray 2.0.

Tig Gen Mov is a generational movement led by the youth of Tigray — united to rebuild our future, reclaim our dignity, and define our destiny.

Who We Are

We are the new generation of Tigray — survivors, thinkers, activists, builders, creators, defenders. We inherit not only the pain of the past but also the responsibility to build a future that our parents were denied. Tig Gen Mov stands for generational justice, collective power, and the right of every Tigrayan youth to shape the next Tigray.

Our Leading Principles

1. Youth Leadership

We believe the youth must lead the political, social, economic, and cultural future of Tigray. No one decides our destiny except us.

2. Generational Responsibility

We are responsible for building Tigray 2.0 — a peaceful, sovereign, thriving nation created by and for the next generation.

3. Right to Rebuild

The youth of Tigray have the right to rebuild what was destroyed — our communities, institutions, identity, and future.

4. Truth & Justice

Our movement stands on truth. The crimes committed against our people must be exposed, documented, and never denied again.

5. Freedom & Sovereignty

A free future is a safe future. We fight for a sovereign Tigray where our people can live without fear or oppression.

6. Peace Through Accountability

There can be no peace without justice. We work for healing, rehabilitation, and the right of survivors to be heard.

7. Innovation & Future Thinking

Tigray must rise through creativity, technology, education, and modern nation-building — led by the youth.

8. Unity of Tegaru

We stand together across the world — Tigray, diaspora, refugees, survivors — united by identity, purpose, and hope.

The Cause We Stand For

Our cause is simple: the youth of Tigray deserve a future free from oppression. We rise because we carry the scars of genocide, the weight of injustice, and the vision of a better nation.

End Generational Suffering

For 50+ years, Tigrayan youth have been targeted by systems of violence and discrimination. We refuse to inherit that suffering.

Rehabilitation & Healing

We fight for mental health support, survivor care, rebuilt infrastructure, and restored dignity for every Tigrayan affected by war.

A Youth-Built Tigray

Tigray must be rebuilt by those who will live in it — the youth. We bring energy, creativity, and new thinking to nation-building.

Political Voice & Representation

No future government of Tigray is legitimate without youth leadership at every level.

Ending Silence

We speak for the voiceless, document the truth, and confront every attempt to erase our people and identity.

Generational Transformation

This movement is not about the past — it is about building the Tigray of tomorrow: peaceful, sovereign, innovative, and youth-led.

We are the generation that survived. We are the generation that rebuilds. We are the generation that will secure Tigray’s future.
